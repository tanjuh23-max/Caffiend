## POSTING TIPS

**Best times to post (UK):**
- 7-9am (morning scroll)
- 12-1pm (lunch)
- 7-9pm (evening)

**First hour matters most:**
- Reply to every comment within 60 minutes of posting
- Ask a question in your caption to drive comments
- Like comments from your account

**Hashtag strategy:**
- Mix 3 big (#coffee, #sleep) + 3 medium (#biohacking, #caffeinetracker) + 2 niche (#caffeinehalflife, #sleepscience)
- Don't use the same hashtag set on every video

**What to do if a video hits >5k views in first 6 hours:**
- Pin a comment with the app link
- Reply to every comment no matter what
- Post a follow-up video within 24 hours while the algorithm is boosting your account
