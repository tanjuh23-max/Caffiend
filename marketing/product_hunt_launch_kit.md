### ALL DAY — Be active on Product Hunt

**Reply to every single comment within 30 minutes.**

When someone comments, respond with something genuine. Examples:

*If they say "this is cool":*
> "Thanks so much! The pharmacokinetics model was the fun part to build — the absorption curve + exponential decay actually predicts your peak to within a few minutes. Glad you like it!"

*If they ask "how is this different from other caffeine apps?":*
> "Every other app just logs what you consumed. Caffiend models what's *currently active in your bloodstream* using the same elimination constant used in clinical pharmacology. So you can see in real-time: right now at 2pm, you have 180mg active, and it'll drop to 50mg by 10pm. No other consumer app does that."

*If they ask about the science:*
> "Happy to nerd out! The model uses a two-phase approach: linear absorption over ~45 mins (gut → bloodstream), then exponential elimination with a 5.7h half-life. The half-life is population-average — actually varies 3-7h based on CYP1A2 enzyme genetics, which is why we have a sensitivity setting."
